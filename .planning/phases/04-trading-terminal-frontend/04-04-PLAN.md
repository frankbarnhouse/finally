   - Full-height right sidebar panel with header "AI ASSISTANT"
   - **Message list:** Scrolling container of messages from chatStore. User messages right-aligned with bg-accent-purple/20 background. Assistant messages left-aligned with bg-terminal-surface background.
   - **Inline action confirmations (UI-11):** When an assistant message has `actions`, render them as small badges/tags below the message text:
     - Trades: "Bought 10 AAPL @ $190.50" or "Sold 5 TSLA @ $220.00" — green badge for buys, red for sells
     - Watchlist changes: "Added PYPL to watchlist" or "Removed NFLX from watchlist" — blue badges
   - **Loading indicator:** When chatStore.loading is true, show a pulsing dot or "Thinking..." indicator in the message area
   - **Input area:** Text input at the bottom with a Send button (bg-accent-purple). Press Enter to send.
   - **Auto-scroll:** After new messages, scroll to bottom. Use `useRef` on the message container and `scrollIntoView` on a bottom sentinel element.

   - Also refresh the watchlist if watchlist_changes are present: call `fetchWatchlist()` or expose a refresh mechanism.
   - Empty state: Show a welcome message "Ask me about your portfolio, or tell me to make a trade!"
